Market

Racks on Racks Incorporated has included a market analysis for the consultation on the metal storage bin rack venture analysis for Marathon Investments, Incorporated. The market has been analyzed for assisting in decisions related to the growth potential which has shown to be expanding at a CAGR of 7.4%. With consideration of the expansion of the bin rack production, the customer profile is defined as relating to homeowners and manufacturers. There is also an assessment of competitors like Loweâ€™s, Home Depot, and Walmart. The manufacturing strategy is to mass produce the bin rack. The analysis includes the SIC code 2542 and NIACS code 337215 for the metal storage bin rack. The seasonality of the market is forecasted based off-of historical trends, which show that the second and third quarter are times of increased production volume.
